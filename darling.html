prabhas

Prabhas (born October 23, 1979, as Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju) is an Indian actor known for his work in Telugu, Hindi, and Tamil cinema. He gained widespread recognition with Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and its sequel Baahubali: The Conclusion (2017), which became one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.
